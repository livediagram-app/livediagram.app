The welcome tour is a guided, interactive introduction to the editor. Rather than showing screenshots, it opens the real panels and menus one at a time, spotlighting each with a highlight ring and a short explanation. It takes about a minute, and you can leave at any point.

## When it's offered

The first diagram you ever create opens with a centred welcome card: **Show me around** starts the tour, **No thanks** dismisses it. The offer appears **once ever**. However you answer (decline, skip midway, or finish), it won't come back on its own, and if you're signed in the answer follows you to every device. You can always [replay it from Settings](#replaying-the-tour).

## What it covers

The tour walks the editor's chrome in a natural order:

1. **The Palette**: the floating panel every shape, arrow, and icon comes from.
2. **Selection modes**: the dropdown that changes what your pointer does (Select, Hand, Eraser, and friends). See [Selection modes](/help/selection-modes/).
3. **Shape categories**: the Palette's category dropdown, from Favourites through Shapes, Tools, Icons, and Technology.
4. **The Explorer**: browsing and organising your diagrams without leaving the editor.
5. **The element menu**: the right-click menu where styling, links, notes, comments, and actions live.
6. **Tabs**: pages within one diagram, plus the **+** button and the tab menu.
7. **Theme & Canvas**: the paintbrush button that restyles the whole tab. (The button lives on every screen size; the tour step itself only runs on desktop.)
8. **Search** (desktop): the Cmd/Ctrl+K panel that finds diagrams, elements, and help articles, and can run actions.

On a phone the tour is slightly shorter: it skips the Theme & Canvas and Search steps.

## During the tour

- Each step shows a **step counter**, a title, a sentence or two of copy, and **Back** / **Next** buttons, with **Skip** always available.
- The highlight ring **never blocks your pointer**: you're free to poke at whatever is highlighted while the step is up. The tour opens real menus, so what you see is exactly what you'll use afterwards.
- Finishing (or skipping) closes anything the tour opened and returns you to a quiet editor, ready to work.

## Replaying the tour

You can rerun the tour whenever you like:

<Step n={1}>Open **Settings** from the editor and find the **Editor** group.</Step>

<Step n={2}>Untick the **Welcome Tour Completed** row.</Step>

<Step n={3}>
  Close Settings. The tour relaunches from the welcome card, exactly like the first run.
</Step>

Finishing the rerun marks it completed again.
