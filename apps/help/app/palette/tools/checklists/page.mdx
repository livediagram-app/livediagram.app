A checklist is a card of checkable to-do rows, a visual home for the task lists that end up on agile boards, retros, and project plans. It is a real canvas element, so you can place, resize, recolour, group, and move it like anything else, and its colours follow the tab's [theme](/help/canvas/themes/) like any shape.

## Adding one

<Step n={1}>Open the floating palette and switch to the **Tools** tab.</Step>
<Step n={2}>
  Click **Checklist** to drop one on the canvas, or drag on the canvas to draw it at a size. It
  starts with a few placeholder rows to overwrite.
</Step>
<Step n={3}>**Click a row's checkbox** to tick it off.</Step>

## Ticking rows

Clicking a checkbox on the canvas toggles that row, no edit mode needed. Done rows tick the box, **strike through** the text, and mute it, and a **done count** (for example "2/5") appears in the card's bottom-right corner once at least one row is done. Every toggle is a single undoable change and **syncs live**, so in a shared session everyone sees a box tick the moment it is clicked.

## Editing the rows

Right-click the checklist, open **Tools**, and choose **Checklist** to edit the rows: retitle any row, toggle its done state, remove it, or **Add row** (up to 30 rows per checklist). Edits commit as normal undoable changes, just like ticking a box.

<Tip>
  Pair a checklist with [assigned actions](/help/collaboration/assigned-actions/): the checklist
  shows the plan at a glance on the board, while assigned actions put a specific person's name on a
  piece of work.
</Tip>
